Chase Hudson aka Lil Huddy

Wikipedia Page Spotify

Chase Hudson, known professionally as Huddy, Lil Huddy, or LilHuddy, is an American social media personality, singer, and actor. He's gained fame from being extremely talented and handsome in his Tik Tok's and single handedly carrying the TikTok collective The Hype House into notoriety. He gained international acclaim for his starring role in season 1 of the 2022 Netflix reality show: Hype House.

Character biography

Season 1 of Hype House

Chase is first introduced in the series during a tour of his mansion. Nicknamed Hype House 2.0 Huddy's Hacienda and The Cheesecake Factory, Chase is shown to be living a well-earned life of leisure. However, this acts as a principal source of conflict with the rest of the cast. Cleverly, Chase has managed to get the Hype House to pay for his $5 million dollar home. Focusing on his massively successful music career, Hudson is at odds with the TikTok house that he helped found as he decides to chase his own bag. Overall, he acts as a misunderstood antagonist to Thomas Petrou throughout the season.

Music Career

Impressively, released his first album in 2021 "Teenage Heartbreak" to critical acclaim. His debut single "21st Century Vampire" quickly gained over 10 million views, with fans and critics alike enjoying the subtle lyrical choices and production value. Respected figures in the industry noted Hudson as an important figure in the 2020s pop punk revival.

Reception

Upon it's January 7th premiere, Hudson's role in the Hype House television series was met with overwhelmingly positive responses. Despite being at odds with a majority of the main cast, viewers were entranced by Chase's reserved nature, artistic passion, and physical beauty. Chase's performance was considered "highly compelling", with many fans being drawn in to his "wholesome" friendship with ex girlfriend Charli D'Amelio.
